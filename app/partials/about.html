About

Doral Properties Real Estate is a company operated by father and son duo William and Daniel Kaskel.

Alfred Kaskel winning an award.

The Kaskel family real estate portfolio dates back to the Great Depression. In 1950, Alfred Kaskel purchased 2,400 acres of swampland in northwest Miami-Dade County. In 1962, Alfred and his wife Doris opened the Doral Hotel and Country Club. Today, this area has become the City of Doral. Interestingly, the name Doral comes from a combination of the founding pair's names: Doris and Alfred.

Daniel Kaskel

Daniel is a 2011 graduate of the University of Arizona with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Business Administration. Daniel started working during in his sophomore year at a student housing apartment complex. After immersing himself into the everyday operations of apartment management, Daniel was promoted from a Community Assistant to Leasing Manager at the onset of his senior year. Since graduating, he has served as an Associate for Doral Properties where he aims to make his mark on the local real estate market.

William Kaskel

William has been an active Real Estate player in South Florida for over 25 years. After graduating magna cum laude with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics from Tufts University, he spent the next two years working for Chase Manhattan Bank N.A. By the second year, he was the commercial real estate department's primary lending officer for the New England region. Shortly after, William moved to South Florida to work for Carol Management Corporation (New York City based parent of all Doral's holdings) as Chief Executive Officer for the Florida division.
